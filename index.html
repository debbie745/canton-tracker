Connect your Google Sheet

  1. Open Canton_Tracker_SheetTemplate.csv in Google Sheets (File → Import → Upload).
  2. In the sheet: File → Share → Publish to web, choose Comma-separated values (.csv), click Publish.
  3. Copy the link it gives you and paste it below. Or paste the normal sheet URL — either works.
